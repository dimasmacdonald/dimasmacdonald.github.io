How do I get started?

To set up a free in-home visit, call us at 812-555-1212 or email at purrfect-petsitters@gmail.com. We’ll come meet you and your pets, take notes on their preferences, and discuss a visit plan

How do I schedule visits?

Once you’ve completed an in-home visit, to schedule pet sitting, just call 812-555-1212.

What are the rates?

$18 per visit.
